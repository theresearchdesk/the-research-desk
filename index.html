Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

What is your typical project turnaround time?

+

Turnaround times vary by project complexity. Academic assignments typically take 5-10 days, while thesis work ranges from 3-8 weeks. Technical projects like websites or automation scripts are usually completed within 2-4 weeks. We always provide a detailed timeline during initial consultation.

Do you offer revisions on completed work?

+

Absolutely. We include two rounds of revisions with every project to ensure complete satisfaction. Additional revisions can be accommodated as needed. Your success is our priority.

What makes TRD different from other service providers?

+

We combine academic rigor with technical expertise. Our team includes researchers with advanced degrees and developers with enterprise experience. We deliver work that meets Global professional standards with attention to detail, originality, and precision.

How do you ensure confidentiality?

+

All projects are protected by strict confidentiality agreements. We use secure communication channels, and never share client information or work samples without explicit permission.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept bank transfers, Wise and Remitly transfers. Payment terms are typically 50% upfront and 50% upon delivery, with flexible arrangements available for long-term projects or returning clients.

Can you handle urgent or rush projects?

+

Yes, we offer expedited service for urgent projects. Rush fees apply based on the complexity and deadline. Contact us immediately with your requirements, and we'll assess feasibility and provide a quote.
