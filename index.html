Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us.

What is your typical project turnaround time?

+

Turnaround varies by complexity. Academic assignments typically take 5–10 days; thesis work ranges from 3–8 weeks. Technical projects like websites or automation scripts are usually completed within 2–4 weeks. We always provide a detailed timeline during initial consultation.

Is the work 100% human-written and AI-free?

+

Absolutely. We have a strict No-AI Policy for all academic work. Every assignment, thesis, and report is written entirely by qualified human experts and passes Turnitin's AI detection checks. We take this commitment seriously — your academic integrity is protected.

Do you offer revisions on completed work?

+

Yes. Every project includes two rounds of revisions to ensure complete satisfaction. Additional revisions can be accommodated as needed. Your success is our priority.

How do you ensure confidentiality?

+

All projects are protected by strict confidentiality. We use secure communication channels and never share client information or work samples without explicit permission. Your privacy is non-negotiable.

Can you handle urgent or rush projects?

+

Yes, we offer expedited service for urgent projects. Rush fees apply based on complexity and deadline. Contact us immediately with your requirements and we'll assess feasibility and provide a quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept bank transfers, Wise, and Remitly. Payment terms are typically 50% upfront and 50% on delivery, with flexible arrangements available for long-term or returning clients.
