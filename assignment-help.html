Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our writing services.

How do I know the work won't contain AI-generated content?

+

Our No-AI Policy is absolute. Every assignment is written from scratch by qualified human writers. We do not use ChatGPT, Jasper, or any other AI writing tool. Your work is checked with Turnitin before delivery — if there are any AI flags (which there won't be), we rewrite it at no charge.

What citation styles do you support?

+

We support all major citation styles including APA (6th and 7th edition), Harvard, MLA, Chicago, OSCOLA, Vancouver, and IEEE. Simply specify your required style when submitting your project.

Can you match my writing style?

+

Yes. Share samples of your previous work when placing your order and our writers will adapt their style to match yours — ensuring consistency and authenticity throughout your academic portfolio.

What if I'm not satisfied with the work delivered?

+

Every project includes two rounds of free revisions. If after revisions you're still not satisfied, we assess the situation and offer either further revisions or a partial refund. Your academic success is our goal.
